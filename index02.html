Requirements to study masters in Canada

A minimum eligibility score in your bachelors (each Canadian institution has an eligibility score). Proof of English language proficiency test score - preferably IELTS or TOEFL. The duly filled application form. A copy of education qualification documents. Statement of purpose. Proof of financial support to study masters in Canada. Some institutions might ask you to take up an evaluation test to verify the education documents submitted.
